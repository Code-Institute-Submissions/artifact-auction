You can add new artifacts to the site through the admin panel. Under the artifact object you can set a reserve price and/or 'buy now' price. Setting a reserve price means that the artifact will not sell if the bids do not exceed the stated price. Setting a buy now price enables purchasers to buy the artifact at the price stated. Please note as bids are received the buy now price will be updated to exceed the current bid by 20%. NB if you do not enter a reserve or buy now price purchasers will be able buy the artifact for 20% more than the initial bid.
